BIOGRAPHY/HISTORY

Liverpool was founded on March 15 1892, by English Businessman John Houlding in a pretty perculair, intriguing chain of events. John Houlding had specifically intended Anfield(Liverpools home ground) to be Everton's permanent home ground, but the Everton committee decided to vacate the stadium, so John Houlding decided he'll form a new football club that'll carry on playing at Anfield; Liverpool Fc; the birth of the greatest English club of all time. The club's first manager was John McKenna, a prominent figure in Liverpool's football scene at the time. Liverpool took no time in making a statement and gaining popularity and success in the football world. They joined the Lancashire League in their debut season and obtained instant success, winning the league title.
